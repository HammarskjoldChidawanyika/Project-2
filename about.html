Our Story

Our story began in 1999 when we
worked together as graphic
designers in a publishing company.
We used to call each other by our
favourite cartoon characters Timon
and Pumba being avid animation
lovers.
Our period of working together was
cut short as opportunities elsewhere
backoned. We kept in touch for the
love of our connection as Timon and
Pumba.

Our competences got resounding
expansion in academia and work
experience. Pumba is more savvy
in webhosting, app development,
SEO, website performance, digital
strategy, video production and is a
part-time lecturer in an internation-
ally recognised university. Timon
studied for BA Hons in Advertising
and Marketing Communications and
went on to do MSc Food Chain Sys-
tems where he specialised in food
packaging. He has worked in

various positions as a packaging
technologist, quality assurance tec-
hnician in the food manufacturing
and distribution industries where
he understood issues around pack-
aging integrity and food safety.
Regardless of the years apart, we
decided to form a business toge-
ther, harnessing the digital age,
and the firm was born as global
with an office in Bedford, UK and
another one in Johannesburg, SA.

We are passionate about giving
our products and services a high
level of excellence. We believe there
is no substitute to quality and as
such our quest for excellence to
our customers is the impetus to
our work ethics. Our positioning is
on excellency of service delivery
at affordable rates. We very much
attest to retaining our current
customers while growing our
clientele.
